**1. Compliance status**

TOPOL.io, s.r.o. websites are created in accordance with Czech Act No.
424/2023 Coll., on accessibility requirements for certain products and
services , Directive (EU) 2019/882 of the European Parliament and of the
Council of 17 April 2019 on accessibility requirements for products and
services, and fully respect the rules for creating an accessible website
defined in the methodological instructions of the Ministry of the
Interior of the Czech Republic. The goal is full compliance with the
internationally recognized standards WCAG 2.1 (Web Content Accessibility
Guidelines), level AA.

Our website meets accessibility requirements, particularly in the
following ways:

- Attributes ARIA correspond to their respective roles;

- ARIA value is not present in the document's body element;

- Elements with attribute role have all required ARIA attributes;

- Attributes ARIA have valid values;

- Attributes ARIA are valid and contain no typos;

- Buttons have accessible names;

- Image elements have ALT attributes;

- The user scalable attribute is not used in the viewport element, and
  the maximum scale attribute is not less than 5;

- ARIA attributes are used correctly, as indicated for the element's
  role;

- Elements with attributes dialog or alertdialog have accessible names;

- Elements use only allowed ARIA attributes;

- Attribute role values are valid;

- Document contains a title element;

- The html element has a lang attribute with a valid value;

- Form elements have associated labels;

- Links have discernible names;

- List item elements are placed within respective parent tags;

- Tap targets are sufficiently large and have sufficient spacing;

- Headings are displayed in a logical sequential order;

- Outdated ARIA roles were not used;

- Image elements do not have ALT attributes with redundant text.
